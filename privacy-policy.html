Privacy Policy - IDefender

At iDefender IT Services Pvt. Ltd., we are committed to protecting the privacy, confidentiality, and security of the personal information entrusted to us by our users, clients, and partners. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you interact with our website, services, or communication channels. By accessing or using our website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this policy and consent to the practices described herein.

1. Collection of Information
‍

We collect information that is necessary to deliver our services effectively and to maintain the security and integrity of our systems. This may include personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, company details, and any information voluntarily submitted through contact forms or inquiries. In addition, we may collect technical and usage data, including IP addresses, browser type, device information, and interaction patterns, to understand how users engage with our website and to improve its performance and security.

‍
‍

2. Use of Information
‍

The information we collect is used strictly for legitimate business and operational purposes. This includes responding to inquiries, providing requested services, managing client relationships, improving our offerings, ensuring platform security, and communicating relevant updates. We may also analyze aggregated and anonymized data to enhance website performance, optimize user experience, and strengthen our cybersecurity posture. Personal information is never used for purposes beyond those outlined without your explicit consent.


‍

3. Information Sharing
‍

iDefender does not sell, rent, or trade personal information. Information may be shared only when necessary to deliver services or comply with legal obligations. This includes sharing limited data with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating our website, managing communications, or delivering technical services, under strict confidentiality agreements. We may also disclose information when required to comply with applicable laws, regulatory requirements, or lawful requests from government or judicial authorities.


‍

4. Data Security
‍

We implement industry-standard technical and organizational security measures to protect personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include access controls, secure infrastructure, encryption practices where appropriate, and continuous monitoring. While we take reasonable steps to safeguard data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage can be guaranteed to be completely secure, and users acknowledge this inherent risk.

‍

5. Your Rights
‍

You have the right to access, review, update, or request deletion of your personal information held by us, subject to applicable legal and contractual obligations. You may also choose to limit or withdraw consent for certain types of communications, including marketing or informational updates. Requests related to personal data rights can be submitted through our contact channels, and we will respond within a reasonable timeframe in accordance with applicable laws.

6. Third-Party Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites or services for informational or operational purposes. iDefender is not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of these external sites. We encourage users to review the privacy policies of any third-party websites they visit, as this Privacy Policy applies solely to information collected through iDefender’s website and services.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy
‍

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in our practices, technologies, legal requirements, or business operations. Continued use of our website following such updates constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
