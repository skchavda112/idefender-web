COMMON QUESTIONS ABOUT OUR PLATFORMS & SERVICES

What types of services does iDefender offer?

iDefender provides enterprise platform development, advisory, and managed services. Our offerings include intelligence platforms, governance and compliance systems, automation engines, commerce platforms, and supporting cloud, security, and operational services.

What do you need from us to start a project?

We begin with a discovery phase to understand your operational landscape, existing systems, governance requirements, and business objectives. Access to key stakeholders and technical context helps us define the right platform approach.

How long does it take to deploy an iDefender platform?

Deployment timelines vary based on platform scope and integration complexity. Initial deployments typically range from a few weeks to a few months, followed by phased scaling and optimization.

What is the cost of your platforms and services?

Pricing is structured based on platform selection, customization, deployment scope, and ongoing operational requirements. We provide transparent, engagement-based pricing after initial assessment.

Do you offer ongoing support and revisions?

Yes. All iDefender platforms are supported through ongoing operations, updates, and enhancements. Our managed services ensure continuous performance, security, and scalability.
